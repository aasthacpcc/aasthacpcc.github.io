# Assignment 1 – Figma Wireframes

## Objective
Create low-fidelity wireframes for a 3–5 screen website or mobile app using Figma.  
This assignment focuses on layout, structure, and content placement — **not colors or final design**.

---

## Requirements

### 1. Create Your Project
1. Go to [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) and create a free account.
2. Create a new file named:  
   `YourName–Wireframes`
3. Choose a frame size: **Mobile** or **Desktop**.

### 2. Create 3–5 Wireframe Screens
Your project must include:

**Required Screen**
- Home Screen

**Additional Screens (choose at least 2)**
- About
- Profile
- Product Page
- Settings
- Contact
- Gallery

### 3. Wireframe Rules

**Your wireframes must include:**
- Gray boxes for images  
- Lines/boxes for text  
- Simple shapes (rectangles, circles)  
- Placeholder buttons  
- Navigation elements  

**Your wireframes must NOT include:**
- Colors  
- Images  
- Detailed styling  
- Prototype links  

Your final wireframes should look like a simple **sketch** of your website or app.

### 4. Submission Requirements
- A **public Figma link** (View-only)  
- Screens arranged clearly in one row or a neat grid  
- A short **2–4 sentence explanation** describing your concept  

---

## Evaluation Criteria
- Completeness of required screens  
- Clear visual hierarchy  
- Consistent layout  
- Proper structure and readability
